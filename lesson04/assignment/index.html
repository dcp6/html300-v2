Hello, and welcome to my blog.

This page will serve as a record of my travels and hiking. I hike mostly in the Seattle area. I've spent a lot of time in the Issaquah Alps recently, but as the season goes on I hope to extend my range out into the wonderful wilderness of the PNW. COVID-19 has shut down most travel, but I look forward to seeing the world some more when the pandemic is over.
